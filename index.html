About Us

Technology has revolutionized the world and holds the key to our future.
We APJ Abdul Kalam Hacker's Academy at Anurag University, initiated coding activities to improve the programming skills of the students and assist them to learn and improve coding skills to excel in Coding Competitions with the help of popular coding platforms.

Objectiive

  • Promote excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education.
  • Advance the development and exchange of relevant knowledge.
  • Gain technical and collaborative problem- solving skills.
  • Be a forum for interaction among the students of the institute.

Platforms

  • CODECHEF
  • HACKEREARTH
  • HACKERRANK
  • TOPCODER

Benifits

In todays rapidly changing environment, programming skills are essential tools that can be utilized and incorporated into various fields and domains. Hence, it becomes absolutely essential to equip young minds with such skills.
Coding Club aims to establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding. The clubs motto is to Create- Build-Innovate.

Events

The event is to make students familiar with coding competitions and inspire them to embrace their brainstorming ability by solving problems. The students will get exposure to industry-related problems and compete to realize their true potential which would also enrich their skills. Our aim through this event is to spread more awareness among students about the importance of solving problems.
